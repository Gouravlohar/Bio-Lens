Common Plant Diseases We Detect

Apple Black Rot

Apple black rot is a disease caused by the fungus Botryosphaeria obtusa that affects apple trees and their fruit.

Powdery Mildew

White powdery spots on leaves, common in various garden plants.

Root Rot

Affects plant roots, causing wilting and yellowing of leaves.
